Keiki on Us! -- from June 26 - July 5, 2020 
Use discount code KEIKI for free Timed Admission rates on kamaʻāina and military Youth tickets!
 

Timed General Admission:

  • To control museum capacity we will be offering 500 daily tickets.
  • There are two time periods to manage the flow of guests during the day: Morning, 9 a.m. - 1 p.m. and Afternoon, 1 p.m. 5 p.m. 
  • If you currently hold a General Admission ticket that is still valid, those tickets will be honored on a space-available basis on the day of attendance. 
  • Members are encouraged to reserve Timed General Admission tickets, at the complimentary price, to minimize personal contact with staff.

Special rates are available below for kama‘āina and military. A Hawai‘i State ID, Hawai‘i Driver’s License, or active military identification is required. This reduced timed admission is also available to students attending colleges and universities in Hawai‘i with valid student ID. Bishop Museum reserves the right to require customers to present collateral documentation establishing residency.
