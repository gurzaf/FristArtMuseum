Become a Friend of Contemporary Art

Friends of Contemporary Art (FoCA) is a social and philanthropic group for those who share a passion for the work of contemporary artists from across the globe. The generous support of FoCA helps the Frist Art Museum organize internationally acclaimed exhibitions and programs that bring the voices of such artists to our community. With your gift of $2,500 (as an addition to your active museum membership), you will receive invitations to meet artists in intimate settings, as well as access to exclusive museum travel opportunities and private FoCA gatherings. To learn more about FoCA benefits, call 615.744.4927 or email sludlam@fristartmuseum.org.

919 Broadway, Nashville, TN 37203-3822 615.244.3340

Accredited by the American Alliance of Museums, the Frist Art Museum is a 501(c)3 nonprofit art exhibition center dedicated to presenting and originating high-quality exhibitions with related educational programs and community outreach activities. The Frist Art Museum offers the finest visual art from local, regional, national, and international sources in a program of changing exhibitions that inspire people through art to look at their world in new ways.
